12 ICONIC WATCHES

The most established brands in history, with a minimum of a century of experience to their names. These watches are renouned as some of the most iconic of all time. This icon set includes watches from notorious brands including Rolex, Omega, Tag Heuer, Cartier, Jaeger-LeCoultre and many more.

THE PROCESS

Starting with a template structure consisting of the height of the watchstrap, the icon faces could then be created to create a consistent but original appearance for each watch icon. The detail of the watchfaces was achieved thanks to smaller but precise grid squares, enabling the complexity of the designs to be achieved. The colours of the icons consist of gradients and shadows to give the watches an authentic, original and realistic look and feel.
